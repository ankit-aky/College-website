  This college also provides the facility for scholarship through Welfare Department.These scholarships are applicable for ST\SC and OBC  ....student.The scholarship can be filled by offline and online.
  The online filling process is give below. Website for scholarship form is

  http://ekalyan.cgg.gov.in

  1. Citizen corner
  2. e-kalayan
  3. post matric within the state
  4. Apply online
  5. Select

  1. Income certificate
  2. Residential certificate
  3. Cast certificate
  4. Bonafied certificate
  5. Marksheet certificate
  6. Hard copy of online application form

  Note: Above certificate is attested through college.
